Etchmiadzin Mother Cathedral

Etchmiadzin Mother Cathedral is considered one of the oldest cathedrals in the world and the spiritual heart of Armenia. It was founded in the 4th century on the site where, according to tradition, Gregory the Illuminator saw a vision of Christ descending.

Over the centuries the cathedral has been rebuilt and strengthened several times, but its core still preserves very early Christian layers and today it remains the seat of the Catholicos of All Armenians.
