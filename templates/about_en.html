What the bot does

The bot shows upcoming events in the city, provides short weather updates, sends basic traffic information and helps you decide where to go in the evening or on weekends.

With simple commands you can get hand-picked lists of movies, theatre plays, concerts and festivals for a given day.

Telegram group and community

The AskYerevan Telegram group is a place where people share their experience, ask questions about Yerevan and help each other discover new spots and activities.

The bot lives inside the same group, so you can use it with short commands without opening a separate app or website.
